About Me

Hello,

My name is Rachel and I am freelance photographer based in Dublin.

Photography has been a passion of mine for several years now. When I'm not working in Milano's I am out with my camera taking pictures or at home editing my work and learing new software. I am a self-taught photographer and love doing photo shoots with my friends, clients and family.

My work includes photoshoots for portraits, fashion, nature, event, new up and coming bands etc.

Please feel free to get in touch for more information and follow me on Instagram #iamrachelhickey.
